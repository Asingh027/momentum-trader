### Caveats
- **2022 bear market is never out-of-sample.** With IS=2yr starting 2021-04-01, the first OOS window
  begins 2023-04-01. The 2022 bear (Jan–Oct 2022) is always inside the IS (training) window. These
  results were never tested on a true bear market OOS. The "bear window" in the table above refers to
  the post-bear 2023 recovery period, not the bear itself.
- All OOS windows (2023–2026) are predominantly bullish or mildly volatile. The strategy has not been
  OOS-validated through a sustained down-trend.
- A 20d trailing stop will whipsaw in choppy markets (mid-2023 sideways grind) — the OOS windows covering
  those periods are the real stress test, and those windows still posted Sharpe >2.
- Higher turnover (20d trail ≈ 23d avg hold vs 49d for 50d trail) roughly doubles execution cost drag.
  The 5 bps slippage model likely understates real friction at this trade frequency.
